PRIVACY POLICY

All Beats by HumanBeat

Last updated: 23 May 2026

1. Introduction

HumanBeat ("we", "our", or "us") operates the All Beats mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

All Beats does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The App does not require account registration, login, or any form of identification.

The App uses the following device functions solely to operate its core features:

  • Device clock and calendar — to calculate moon phase alignment times and display countdown timers.
  • Vibration — to provide haptic feedback when the user interacts with the App.
  • Notifications — to send local reminders before moon phase alignment events.

All of the above functions operate entirely on your device. No data is sent to our servers or any third party as a result of these functions.

3. Data Storage

All Beats does not maintain any servers, databases, or cloud storage. All App functionality runs locally on your device. No user data is stored externally.

4. Third-Party Services

The App contains a link to an external donation page operated by Buy Me a Coffee (buymeacoffee.com). This link opens an external website in your browser. We do not control or have access to any data collected by Buy Me a Coffee. Users who choose to visit this page are subject to Buy Me a Coffee's own Privacy Policy, available at buymeacoffee.com.

5. Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications to remind users of upcoming moon phase alignments. These notifications are generated and stored entirely on your device. No notification content or data is transmitted externally. You may disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

All Beats is suitable for users of all ages. The App does not collect any personal information from any user, including children under the age of 13, and is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this document. We encourage users to review this page periodically.
